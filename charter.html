Pandamonium is a member-driven MMO gaming community with core values of inclusivity, equity, and accountability. We are comprised of a richly diverse collection of people from all walks of life, including many marginalized groups, who are committed to having fun. At our center are real-life and online friends who have been gaming together since 2004. Our goal is to provide a safe environment free of drama, where all members show each other mutual respect.

Age Requirement
A minimum age of 18 years is required for acceptance into Pandamonium. By submitting an application to join the community, the applicant is declaring that they meet the age requirement. No exceptions to this rule will be made.

“There are no exceptions to the rule that everybody likes to be an exception to the rule.”

Diversity
Our members represent differences in race, age, gender, politics, religion, and sexual orientation. Those inclined towards bigoted, racist, homophobic, transphobic, xenophobic, or misogynistic behavior need not apply. You wouldn't like it here, and we wouldn't like to have you.

Honor
Pandamonium members are expected to conduct themselves in a manner which is becoming to them and to the community. Inflammatory or insulting comments, and rude or petty behavior have no place in game chat, voice chat, Discord text chat, or interactive game forums and will be dealt with immediately.

“Be honorable yourself if you wish to associate with honorable people.”

Commitment
Real life takes precedence over gaming. All Pandamonium members will be respectful of others' real life situations, circumstances, and emergencies. That being said, we expect members to honor their commitments. Before a member agrees to attend an event they should realistically determine if they will actually be able to fulfill the commitment. Once a commitment is made, members are expected to show up on time and be prepared. When circumstances arise that prevent fulfillment of a commitment, members are expected to let the appropriate event leader know as soon as possible.

“Unless commitment is made, there are only promises and hopes; but no plans.”

Integrity
It is expected that all members will abide by the game Terms of Service and Rules of Conduct at all times. Specifically, the use of 3rd party hacks and/or cheats will result in immediate removal from the community.

“There can be no friendship without confidence, and no confidence without integrity.”

Loyalty
All members of Pandamonium are expected to abide by this Charter. Members are encouraged to invite other players whom they believe will be an asset to the community to submit an application via the Pandamonium Discord (#apply).

“The greater the loyalty of a group toward the group, the greater is the motivation among the members to achieve the goals of the group, and the greater the probability that the group will achieve its goals.”

Official Teams
Pandamonium is primarily a casual community. However, we have solid procedures in place to satisfy those individuals interested in endgame raiding, ranked PvP, and other organized events. The Official Teams page contains details on how our teams function within the underlying community structure (as well as an overview of the team creation process).

Website/Discord
The community maintains this website as a recruitment tool and the Discord server as a communication tool for its members. You can check for announcements, upcoming events, and general chat. A wealth of information may be found on the website and within Discord. Members are encouraged to share their knowledge and experiences with others.

"Communication leads to community, that is, to understanding, intimacy and mutual valuing."

Live Streaming
Individuals interested in live streaming in-game content that includes fellow community members can do so only with the permission of those sharing their voice channel or in-game group. Planned community events that will be live streamed must be advertised as such. This must include whether the event will be permanently saved on the Twitch website. We have posted Streaming Guidelines to assist those interested in getting started with streaming.

"Privacy matters; privacy is what allows us to determine who we are and who we want to be."

Member Inactivity
All members are expected to be active both in-game and on Discord. Failure to log-in to any of the chapters for more than 120 days may result in removal from the community. In order to rejoin Pandamonium, the inactive individual must reapply for membership. This includes Members who previously chose to leave the community. If you permanently leave the Pandamonium Discord server we will view this as a signal of your decision to leave the community. Any guilded characters will be removed. Please keep in mind that those who leave the community multiple times (either due to inactivity or of your own volition) are unlikely to be welcome back more than once. Note that some games may have a shorter inactivity timer due to in-game limitations (e.g., New World is 60 days).

Alternate Characters
Players that have achieved the Member rank and above are invited to bring alternate characters into any active, legacy, or pilot chapter. Alternate characters will be held to the same inactivity requirement as main characters.

Guild Tags
Pandamonium, if available, is our guild/FC/company name. If it is not available or we require multiple entities for a particular game (e.g., because we have an opposite faction entity or because of in-game member limitations), we also use Pandaloons and Pandalysium.

Any individual with the rank of Member and higher wishing to tag an alternate character need merely ask one of the Chapter Officers or Game Managers for an invite to any of the guilds. Please note that only current members of Pandamonium will be allowed into any of our guilds/FCs/companies, and the Pandamonium Charter applies equally to all. Though the rank structures are identical, opposite faction guilds, in chapters where they exist, are intended to merely be alt guilds. This means that our primary focus is and will always be the primary faction guild.

In New World, the companies on Lilith and Maramma are membership entities. Members may have a character in the company on either server or both.

Chapters
Active Chapters
An Active Chapter is an MMO that Pandamonium has an active presence in. It has the full use of resources within our gaming community, including recruitment, leadership manpower, and more active Discord channels to facilitate its growth and prosperity. A game becomes an Active Chapter if there is sufficient community interest, suitable individuals willing to accept Chapter Officer roles, and the Leadership Team agrees with its transition from a Pilot/Legacy status. There are no specific numbers required since Pandamonium's community population will fluctuate over time and the key criteria is in-game activity.

Currently, we have three Active Chapters: Final Fantasy XIV Online, New World, and Palia.

Legacy Chapters
A Legacy Chapter is an MMO that Pandamonium has a small presence in which was previously active and may be resurrected at some future point. For now, it is relatively quiet. It does not have a leadership structure like an Active Chapter. Rather, the in-game guild leader role is held by a trustworthy community member: the Game Manager. Holding a Game Manager role does not alone make you a member of the community Leadership Team. Legacy Chapters will have no active recruitment, as it is merely intended to allow for casual gameplay among existing members of the Pandamonium community. However, applications through a Legacy Chapter are allowed. A Legacy Chapter can be upgraded to an Active Chapter at any time if the Leadership Team determines that it qualifies.

Currently, we have three Legacy Chapters: EverQuest II, Star Wars: The Old Republic, and World of Warcraft.

Pilot Chapters
A Pilot Chapter is an MMO that Pandamonium is testing out to see if there is enough community interest to sustain. Typically, it is formed at game release. It does not have a standard leadership structure like an Active Chapter. Rather, existing Leadership Team members and/or Life Members take on the role of mentors to get things organized and vet potential future Chapter Officers. Applications through a Pilot Chapter are allowed and a Pilot Chapter can be converted to an Active Chapter at any time once it has its own self-sustaining/non-transitional officer team and a reasonable number of active members. Alternatively, a Pilot Chapter can be closed if it is deemed to be a failed experiment.

Currently, we have no Pilot Chapters.

Ranks
Apprentice
An Apprentice is an individual who has applied for membership through the required channels, has agreed to abide by the published community charter, and has been welcomed into Pandamonium for a trial period during which time the Apprentice will be evaluated for compatibility and integrity. Apprentices may participate in all events for which they are geared and eligible. An Apprentice may be promoted to full membership upon recommendation by any member in good standing and after a period of no less than 2 weeks.

Member
A Member is an individual who has successfully completed the recruitment phase and been approved for full membership by the leadership team. The Member will have full access to all Pandamonium events. A Member may choose to bring their alternate characters into any Active, Legacy, or Pilot Chapter. If a Member removes their toons from all Chapters before reaching Life Member rank, they will be removed from the Pandamonium Discord server and must reapply for membership should they wish to return.

Life Member
A Life Member is an esteemed member who has demonstrated long-standing commitment and dedication to the community. Membership is retained even though they may no longer be tagged in a Pandamonium chapter as long as they remain active on Discord (a negative parting of ways being the obvious exception). Chapter inactivity timers will not be applied to Life Members as long as there is room within the in-game guild/company/FC. Though the bestowing of this rank is sometimes subjective and dealt with on a case-by-case basis, the general guidelines include being a community member for 18+ months and generally active in voice and text chat on Pandamonium's Discord server. If a Life Member leaves the Pandamonium Discord server, that is reflective of a decision to leave the community and they must reapply for membership should they wish to return.

Team Leader
A Team Leader is a Member that also functions as a raid team leader. They coordinate and spearhead forces, maintaining a balanced team to handle the toughest fights. To aid them, Team Leaders have access (if available) to a dedicated raiding bank which assists with logistical support and stocking mission critical supplies.

Moderator
A Moderator is responsible for Discord moderation. They monitor the interchange of contributors and make decisions regarding content. If the tone becomes hostile or starts to move in the direction of personal attacks, a Moderator has discretion to intervene in the discussion to prevent heated, inappropriate exchanges. Conversely, topics they feel deserve further examination can be pinned for easier access. They are responsible for processing community applications and membership administration duties. Moderators are selected on an as-needed basis.

Game Manager
A Game Manager is responsible for all in-game administration for the Legacy or Pilot Chapter that they oversee. A Game Manager must be approved by the Community Managers.

Chapter Officer
A Chapter Officer is an active participant within the game. They are involved in general community oversight and policy implementation. Though specific duties vary from person-to-person and chapter-to-chapter, they may include inviting and welcoming new apprentices, assisting members with general information, providing valuable and significant content to our Discord, organizing guild events, and creating a positive environment in guild and voice chat. Chapter Officers are selected on an as-needed basis.

Chapter Leader
A Chapter Leader is responsible for the organization and in-game administration of the chapter they oversee. Working with their officer teams, they are the ultimate decision-makers for the chapter; within the charter's guidelines. If the game requires a single GM, they fill this role. More than one Chapter Leader can exist for a chapter, if necessary.

Community Manager
A Community Manager coordinates community policy creation. These individuals play a key role in general community management by enforcing such policies, setting community goals, and overseeing the selection of new chapters. The majority of our Community Managers have been managing community activities together and keeping the boat afloat since 2004.
