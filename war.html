New World War Information
Faction Influence
Influence is gained by doing and turning in Faction PvP quests in a region or taking the fort and defending it. When a settlement has a new owner, a single faction turn-in by attackers may be much less than 1%. Each day they hold the settlement, the gains increase for attackers a small amount. Meanwhile, defenders no longer have influence scaling. In other words, the longer a territory is held, the easier it is to push faction against it, but it remains fairly easy to counter that influence by defenders. (Adjustments have been made to the scaling, so we will have to check the amounts)

When a faction pushes influence to 100%, that faction is able to declare war. First to declare will be the Company that has the largest contribution to that 100%, then all other contributors greater than 10%. To declare war, you bring up the map, click on the settlement, scroll down, click Declare War, and pay the amount (i.e., 7.5k, 10k, or 15k depending on what tier Attack fort you wish). Then, after the selection period, a lottery occurs and the Vanguard is determined and charged the amount. Others are refunded.

After a war is declared, we will need to determine which generals will run it, selected from those who are available at that time. The one or two generals then sign up to that war and get added to the roster and promoted by a Consul or Governor to War Leader(s).

The war will always happen 24 - 48 hours after it is declared and occur at the war window of the defending company. The rule is at the war window, at least 1 day. So if Declared just at our war window, the war will occur in 24 hours. If Declared just after our war window, it will be in 48 hours.

 War Roster Selection

During the first 12 hours, the War Leaders will fill Panda company members to roughly half the roster, meeting adequate force levels of healers, tanks, ranged, melee, and siege (based on the details submitted on the Panda War Participants form). If more signups are available than half, the War Leaders will select the highest levels in each category. If multiple simultaneous wars are occuring, the War Leaders will split according to signups in each war and ask for transfers accordingly (i.e., they sign up in the other war).

During the second 12 hours, the War Leaders will fill fellow Syndicate members accordingly, meeting adequate force levels of healers, tanks, ranged, melee, and siege (check Syndcate roster spreadsheet for their specs and gear score). If not on the spreadsheet populated by the Panda War Participants form, send them multiple messages to accertain level, specs, gear score, etc. If no answer, put them on standby. Exchange Discord info, let them know to check into our Discord (https://discord.gg/pandaguild/) 30 minutes before the war.

During the last 1 hour, the War Leaders fill any empty slots with the best candidates available.

During the last 1/2 hour, the War Leaders fill any red marked/offline characters with the best candidates available.

At 30 minutes before, gather in the appropriate Discord war channel. Go over the plan and assignments.



At 15 minutes before, all hit F1 to enter War. Some will fail to load in and automatic backfill may occur. Send them our Discord info. Have everyone filter chat to Army. Say this in Discord and Army and Area. Try to refill any odd backfills in critical assignments. Send them the Discord info again.

 War Sign-Up Process

  1. Sign up at the war board in-game in the appropriate settlement.
  2. Complete the Panda War Participants form. If you have done so previously, no need to resubmit unless your information has changed.
  3. Join the Pandamonium Discord server: https://discord.gg/pandaguild
r> Settlement War FAQ

Why was I added and then removed from the War roster?
We are sorry if this happened. It's sometimes necessary due to the formation process. If we don't do our best to win the war, there would be thousands of angry Syndicate members. Frequently, if this happens at the half hour, it is because you are showing as offline but you are actually online.

I hit F1, but am now flickering or didn't load into the War or cannot move. What now?
Report it to us so we can remove and try to re-add you. This frequently happens to 2-6 players per War and may be due to server error or extreme lag. For this reason, try to not load in from the middle of Everfall or Windsward.

I am a non-Panda not being selected for Wars but want to be involved. How can I do that?
You and your company could start wars on Attack, become Vanguard, and much of the Syndicate would sign up. Then you could determine the selection process, and, if we win, your company gains control of the settlement.
